Dannisha & Kemar

Are getting Married in

He asked her & she said yes!

Kemar proposed to Dannisha during a planned photo shoot outdoors in one of the most beautiful botanical gardens. Since Dannisha always expressed her love for nature and greenery, it was the ideal spot to pop the big question. The photographer started to snap a few random pictures while Kemar tugged her hand and walked her to the wonderfully decorated spot. Surprise! Shouted a few family members who popped out of their hiding place. At that moment, Dannisha could not hide her emotions as she walked in front of a large prop with the words “Marry Me?” Kemar immediately went down on his knee while opening a neat little ring box as the saxophonist began to play one of her favorite songs (All my life by K-Ci & JoJo). Dannisha said “oh my gosh, baby, you did all this for me?” as she wiped tears of joy from her eyes. She said yes.
